How to Play

1

Read the Trivia Question

Each equation has a trivia question. The answer is always a number that you need to place in the equation.

2

Place Numbers in Blanks

Click a number from the pool at the bottom, then click a blank space in an equation to place it.

3

Match Column Totals

Numbers in each column must add up to the target shown at the top. This is the key constraint!

4

Complete All Equations

Make sure each equation is mathematically correct, then submit to win and see the confetti!
